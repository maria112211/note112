NoTell is a cutting-edge security awareness company that utilizes AI technology to guide users in real-time to make correct security decisions and prevent phishing attacks. Unlike traditional companies that rely on static content, NoTell investigates and adds context to suspicious emails, text messages email ids, and URLs, providing personalized training on-the-fly.

Traditional cybersecurity training is based on static content. However, NoTell's training is dynamic and is adjusted in real-time based on the security threats that occur within an organization. This means that our training is always up-to-date, fully personalized, and relevant to the current threat landscape, providing users with the most current information and guidance.

By providing real-time guidance, NoTell's training helps users to quickly and correctly respond to incidents, minimizing the disruption caused by security threats. Additionally, by providing ongoing, relevant training, our system can help to reduce the number of security incidents and the associated downtime, ultimately resulting in increased productivity for your organization.
